About IPDCEC

A global platform for future chemical engineers

IPDCEC is an international chemical plant design competition organized by the Chemical Engineering Department, Faculty of Engineering, Universitas Malikussaleh. This competition challenges students to design comprehensive, safe, efficient, and sustainable chemical plants.

Global Participation

Open to active students from Chemical Engineering and other majors from universities around the world, as long as they can follow the official guidebook requirements and required report format.

Open Across Majors

Participants from non-Chemical Engineering departments are welcome, provided they are able to prepare the plant design report according to the scope, structure, and standards requested in the official guidebook.

Innovative Solutions

Encouraging process innovation, engineering creativity, and practical industrial design.

Sustainable Future

Focused on efficient process design, energy optimization, safety, economics, and environmental responsibility.

Host Institution

Universitas Malikussaleh & Chemical Engineering Program

IPDCEC 2026 is hosted by Universitas Malikussaleh through the Chemical Engineering academic environment, supporting students in process design, simulation, innovation, and sustainable chemical industry development.

Universitas Malikussaleh

Universitas Malikussaleh is a public university in Aceh, Indonesia, with academic activities centered across campuses including Reuleut, Bukit Indah, and Lhokseumawe. As the host institution, Universitas Malikussaleh provides an academic environment that connects engineering education, local industrial context, research, and community development.

The IPDCEC 2026 final stage will be held in the Universitas Malikussaleh academic environment in Lhokseumawe, Aceh, giving participants the opportunity to experience both chemical engineering learning and the local culture of Aceh.

Program Studi Teknik Kimia

The Chemical Engineering Study Program, Faculty of Engineering, Universitas Malikussaleh prepares students with competencies in industrial process design, process simulation, sustainable production, energy, safety, environmental responsibility, and chemical plant engineering.

Akreditasi Unggul

Excellent Accreditation Status

Program Studi Teknik Kimia Universitas Malikussaleh has obtained the “Unggul” accreditation rank from LAM Teknik based on SK No. 0109/SK/LAM Teknik/AS/IV/2026. The accreditation status is valid from 21 April 2026 to 20 April 2031.

Why It Matters for IPDCEC 2026

This accreditation strengthens the academic credibility of the host program and supports IPDCEC 2026 as an international competition focused on plant design, process innovation, sustainability, and professional chemical engineering standards.

Theme & Subtheme

Competition Theme

“Perancangan Pabrik Kimia Berbasis Industri untuk Masa Depan Global yang Berkelanjutan”

01. Cleaner Energy from Natural Gas

Utilization of natural gas as a cleaner energy source through process optimization and emission reduction.

  • Hydrogen (H2)
  • DME
  • Methanol
  • SNG
  • GTL fuels

02. Value-Added Chemical Products

Downstream processing of natural gas into value-added chemical products for sustainable and eco-friendly industry.

  • Ammonia and Urea
  • Olefin and Polymers
  • Formaldehyde
  • Acetic Acid
  • Petrochemical derivatives

Competition Atmosphere

A Professional Stage for Future Chemical Engineers

IPDCEC 2026 is designed to provide an international-standard presentation atmosphere where teams present their chemical plant design work in front of judges, academics, and participants.

Track Record & Achievements

Experience in National and International Plant Design Competitions

The Chemical Engineering Study Program of Universitas Malikussaleh has continuously developed academic competition activities and achievements in plant design engineering.

National Competition Experience

Plant Design Competition at National Scale

Before organizing IPDCEC 2026 as an international competition, the Chemical Engineering Study Program of Universitas Malikussaleh had already organized Plant Design Competition activities at the national level involving students from universities across Indonesia.

National Champion Achievement

1st Place Winner at CPSC 2025 – Universitas Riau

In 2025, the Chemical Engineering Study Program of Universitas Malikussaleh achieved 1st Place in the national Chemical Plant Design Competition (CPSC) organized by the Department of Chemical Engineering, Universitas Riau (UNRI), demonstrating strong academic capability and competitiveness in plant design engineering.

Aceh Culinary Experience

Try Mie Aceh, the Iconic Spiced Noodle of Aceh

Mie Aceh is one of Aceh's signature dishes, made with thick yellow noodles, bold spices, and toppings such as beef, goat meat, shrimp, crab, or other seafood. It is commonly served fried, stir-fried with a little sauce, or as a rich soupy noodle dish.

Travel & Transportation

How to Get to Lhokseumawe, Aceh

IPDCEC 2026 will be held in Lhokseumawe, Aceh. Participants can reach the city by air through Malikus Saleh Airport (LSW), or by land from Medan and Banda Aceh using intercity buses, travel cars, or private transport.

For international participants, the easiest route is usually to fly to Jakarta, Kuala Lumpur, Medan, or Banda Aceh first, then continue to Lhokseumawe by domestic flight or land transportation.

Nearest Airport: LSW Via Medan Kualanamu Bus / Travel Car Available
Route to Competition Venue Open Malikus Saleh Airport Map

By Air: Fly to Lhokseumawe

Malikus Saleh Airport (LSW) serves the Lhokseumawe area. Flight availability may change, so participants should check the latest schedule from airline or ticketing platforms before departure.

View LSW Airport

By Land from Medan

Participants arriving at Kualanamu International Airport (Medan) can continue to Lhokseumawe by bus, travel car, or private car. This route is commonly used by visitors entering Aceh from North Sumatra.

Route from Kualanamu

By Land from Banda Aceh

Visitors who land at Sultan Iskandar Muda International Airport in Banda Aceh can travel east to Lhokseumawe by bus, shuttle, travel car, taxi, or private transport.

Route from Banda Aceh Airport

Travel Tips for Participants

Please book flights and land transportation early, especially near the final presentation dates. For comfort and safety, international participants are encouraged to share their arrival plan with the committee so local guidance can be provided when needed. Always confirm current ticket availability, route, and travel duration before departure.
