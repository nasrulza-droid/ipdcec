About the Competition

The International Plant Design Competition invites university students to propose creative plant design concepts that address real-world engineering, sustainability, and industrial challenges.

Who Can Join?

Undergraduate or graduate students from universities, individually or in teams.

Team Size

Each team may consist of up to 4 members and 1 supervisor.

Submission

Participants submit a proposal in PDF format through the official registration form.
